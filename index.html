Our Story

At Josh's Coffee Shop, we believe a great cup of coffee is the start of a great day. Our journey began with a simple passion for perfect coffee, a passion that has driven us to source the finest beans from around the world. We are committed to a sustainable and transparent process, ensuring every bean is ethically and responsibly grown.

Our cafe is more than just a place to grab a coffee; it's a community hub where locals and visitors can relax, connect, and enjoy a moment of tranquility. We invite you to be a part of our story.

Our Roasting Process

1. Sourcing

We work directly with farmers to select and import the highest quality green coffee beans.

2. Roasting

Our skilled roasters carefully monitor each batch to unlock its unique and complex flavor profile.

3. Brewing

Every cup is brewed fresh to order, ensuring you get the perfect taste and aroma with every sip.
